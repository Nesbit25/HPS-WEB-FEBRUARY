## ⚡ QUICK UPLOAD STEPS

### 1. Click the GitHub URL above for the folder you want to update

### 2. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"

### 3. Drag your images (with EXACT file names)

### 4. Click "Commit changes"

### 5. Wait 2-3 minutes for automatic deployment

### 6. Refresh your browser (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)

## ⚠️ FILE NAMING RULES

✅ **CORRECT:**
- `hero-slide-1.jpg`
- `hero-slide-2.jpg`
- `hero-slide-3.jpg`
- `logo-main.svg`

❌ **WRONG:**
- `Hero-Slide-1.jpg` (wrong capitalization)
- `hero_slide_1.jpg` (underscores instead of dashes)
- `hero-slide-01.jpg` (zero-padded numbers)
- `Logo-Main.svg` (wrong capitalization)
- `hero slide 1.jpg` (spaces)

**File names are case-sensitive and must match exactly!**
