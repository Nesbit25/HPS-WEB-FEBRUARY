## 🤖 **Optimizing for Google AI Overviews**

### **What Makes Content AI-Overview Friendly:**

1. **Answer Questions Directly**
   - Add FAQ sections to each procedure page
   - Start answers with the question
   - Example: "How long does rhinoplasty recovery take? Most patients return to work within 7-10 days..."

2. **Use Lists & Tables**
   - AI loves structured, scannable content
   - Add bulleted benefit lists
   - Create comparison tables (e.g., "Breast Augmentation Options")

3. **Include Statistics & Data**
   - "Over 15 years of experience"
   - "Thousands of successful procedures"
   - "Board certified by 2 major organizations"

4. **Local Context**
   - Mention Baton Rouge in content naturally
   - Reference Louisiana-specific info
   - Include neighborhood names if relevant

5. **E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust)**
   - Highlight Dr. Hanemann's credentials prominently
   - Mention LSU and Tulane faculty positions
   - Include board certifications
   - Show real patient testimonials

## 🎓 **Medical SEO Best Practices**

1. **YMYL (Your Money Your Life) Content**
   - Google scrutinizes medical content heavily
   - Cite medical sources
   - Be factually accurate
   - Don't make exaggerated claims

2. **Patient Privacy**
   - Always get consent for before/after photos
   - Use generic patient names ("Patient A")
   - Follow HIPAA guidelines

3. **Medical Disclaimers**
   - "Results may vary"
   - "Individual results depend on many factors"
   - "Not medical advice - consultation required"
